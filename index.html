Hey there! Welcome to an experimental way of exploring 3D maps

The aim here is to control the 3D globe using your eyes... that's right! You will train the program to learn your eye movements by clicking on boxes that will appear. Please look at them whislt you click. This will in turn allow you to move its directions by looking to the edge of the screens in the direction you wish to move. This is still a beta project. Feedback is apreciated! Ready to go?
